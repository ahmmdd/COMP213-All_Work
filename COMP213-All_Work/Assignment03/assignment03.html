Huawei is one of the leading global information and communications technology (ICT) solutions providers in Canada. It first established a Canadian office in 2008 in Markham, Ontario – just north of the City of Toronto.

Since that time, Huawei Canada has grown to over 450 employees. In addition to the corporate headquarters in Markham, Huawei operates a major Research & Development (R&D) Centre in Ottawa, Ontario, and has offices in Montreal and Edmonton.

Huawei’s core businesses in Canada range from Telecom Network Infrastructure, Applications and Software, Professional Services, to Enterprise and Devices. Huawei works with many of Canada’s leading telecommunications service providers, including TELUS, Bell, Sasktel, WIND Mobile and Videotron.

We are also proud of our diverse workforce. 90% of Huawei Canada’s employees have been hired locally. In April 2012, we have been recognized by the Toronto Region Immigrant Economic Council for our efforts to recruit and hire skilled new immigrants.
